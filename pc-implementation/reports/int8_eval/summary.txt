PC MODEL EVALUATION — float vs MAX78000-realistic int8 PTQ
=========================================================================================================
Quantization pipeline (training/quantize.py::quantize_model_ptq):
  0. BN fold     : every Conv2d→BatchNorm2d pair fused into
                   the conv weight/bias (same step the MAX78000
                   does via bn_fuser_v2.py at device synthesis)
  1. weight quant: symmetric per-tensor int8, power-of-two=True
                   scale = max(|w_folded|) / 127
  2. activations : calibrated over 20 train batches (~5120 imgs)
  3. fake-quant  : pre-forward hooks round each Conv/Linear input
                   to the int8 grid before the multiply

This matches what the MAX78000 actually deploys — fp32 models
will show a real PTQ drop, QAT models should recover most of it.

-------------

  MACs/inf       : multiply-accumulate operations per inference
